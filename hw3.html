A brief look at the everlasting influence that is Led Zeppelin

Led Zeppelin were an English rock band formed in 1968. Originally influenced by blues and folk music, their heavy, guitar-driven sound has credited them as the progenitors to hard rock and heavy metal. With their influence spanning through multiple genres of music, Led Zeppelin has been credited as significantly impacting the nature of the music industry, and are widely regarded as one the most influential groups of the 20th century.

Meet The Band:

  • Robert Plant - Lead Vocals
  • Jimmy Page - Lead Guitar
  • John Paul Jones - Bassist/Keyboard
  • John Bonham - Drums
  • Pete Grant - Music Manager
