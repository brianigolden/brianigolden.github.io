Sites To Visit in San Francisco

San Francisco has long been a sought after destination for out of towners from all over the globe to come visit. The cities beutiful landmarks, rich cultural diversity, and deep historical roots make it hard to not love everything about it.


Some of the most popular tourist attractions attract thousands of visitors each year, and serve as some of San Francisco's most identifiable landmarks.

Notable Landmarks:

  • Golden Gate Bridge
  • Lombard Street
  • Alcatraz
  • Mission Dolores
  • Golden Gate Park
  • Grace Cathedral
  • Aquarium of The Bay
